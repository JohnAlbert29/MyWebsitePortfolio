Capstone Project

Academic Thesis

Talent Track: Digital Recruitment System

Talent Track is our Capstone Project developed by Christian Montero and myself. It revolutionizes Lazada's expediter hiring process by implementing automation and digital interaction, reducing hiring time by 40% and improving candidate experience.

Thesis Abstract

This study aimed to develop a digital recruitment system to streamline Lazada's expediter hiring process. The system integrates automated CV screening, interview scheduling with calendar synchronization, digital testing modules, and real-time progress tracking.

Key Features:

Website for Applicant
CV submission & application tracking with real-time notifications
Interview scheduling with Calendar API's
Digital tutorials, online exams, and video interview capabilities
Auto-generated reports and AI-powered candidate evaluations

Technologies Used:

Tailwind CSS Node.js Next.js PostgreSQL Nodemailer React Email

Academic Project

E-Commerce Platform for Quiapo Local Businesses

Developed as our final project in Website Development, this e-commerce platform supports three unique local businesses in Quiapo, Manila, helping them establish an online presence and reach more customers.

Project Overview

This platform was designed to help traditional brick-and-mortar stores in Quiapo transition to digital commerce. The system features inventory management, secure payment processing, and a unified storefront for three distinct businesses while maintaining their individual brand identities.

Businesses Served:

The Powerhouse - Electronics and gadgets retailer with over 200 products
Bike Manila - Bicycle shop specializing in urban cycling solutions
Eyewear Glass - Optical shop offering prescription and fashion eyewear

Technologies Used:

React.js CSS3 HTML5 JavaScript JSX Firebase
